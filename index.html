    Defining the Art of Gastronomy with Passion.

    For over two decades, we have been more than just a kitchen; we have been a destination for food enthusiasts who seek perfection in every bite.

    • Hand-picked seasonal ingredients sourced directly from local organic farms.
    • Exclusive recipes passed down through generations, refined with a modern culinary twist.
    • An elegant atmosphere designed to turn every meal into a cherished memory.

    We believe that great food is the soul of every celebration. Our mission is to provide an unparalleled dining experience where world-class service meets exquisite flavors. Join us and discover why we are the city's most beloved culinary landmark.

    The Signature Flame-Grilled Ribeye

    A masterpiece of flavor, seasoned with our secret spice rub and grilled to smoky perfection.

    Our premium cuts of beef are aged for 21 days to ensure maximum tenderness. Served with a side of garlic mashed potatoes and roasted seasonal vegetables, finished with a drizzle of our signature peppercorn sauce.

    Authentic Italian Fettuccine

    Freshly handmade pasta tossed in a rich, creamy Alfredo sauce with sun-dried tomatoes.

    We import our flour directly from Italy to give you the most authentic texture. This dish is topped with grilled herb-chicken and aged Parmesan cheese, bringing the taste of Rome straight to your table.

    Ocean's Finest Seafood Platter

    A luxurious selection of grilled lobster, jumbo prawns, and buttered scallops.

    Caught fresh daily, our seafood is lightly seasoned with lemon zest and Mediterranean sea salt. It’s the perfect choice for seafood lovers looking for a light yet incredibly flavorful meal.

    East-West Fusion Delight

    A creative blend of traditional Eastern spices with Western culinary techniques.

    Experience the bold flavors of the East integrated into a modern gourmet presentation. Our Fusion Delight changes weekly, featuring seasonal surprises curated by our executive chef.

    The Chocolate Symphony

    A triple-layered dark chocolate lava cake served with vanilla bean gelato.

    Indulge in the richness of Belgian chocolate that melts with every spoonful. This dessert is designed to provide a perfect sweet ending to your luxurious dining experience at Delicious.
